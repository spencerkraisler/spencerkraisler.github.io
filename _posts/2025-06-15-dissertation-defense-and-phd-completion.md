I successfully defended my PhD dissertation, **Controller Synthesis through Riemannian Optimization**, at the University of Washington.

### What problem was this about?
Many control and trajectory optimization methods are built in coordinates that ignore underlying geometric structure, which can lead to redundancy and weaker numerical behavior.

### What was the main contribution?
The dissertation develops intrinsic geometric optimization methods for both closed-loop controller synthesis and open-loop trajectory optimization on manifolds.

### Why it matters
The resulting methods are both theoretically principled and computationally practical, with direct relevance to learning-based control and autonomous aerospace systems.
